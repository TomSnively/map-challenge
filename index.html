Map Challenge

Setup

Get your map ready! I suggest this map from National Geographic:

Hang it on your wall, stand in front of it, have this web app ready, and play!

The Game

When you begin, you will have five geographical items (one country, one US state, one city, one body of water and one other item.

Find them all in seconds to beat the clock. Good luck!


Click on each item when you find it on your map.
